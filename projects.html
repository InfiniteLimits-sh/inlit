Tour August 2025

KWOON · 6 Cities

KWOON is a post-rock band from France known for intense live energy and genre-blending sound. In 2025, they released their new album Odyssey. InfiniteLimits organized and executed their album tour in China, across 6 cities with full technical production and merchandise support.

Beijing Shanghai Guangzhou Shenzhen Hangzhou Nanjing

Tour photos / 巡演照片

Highlights / 项目亮点

The tour featured professional sound and lighting engineers throughout, who worked with the band on effects before each show, and received widespread acclaim. With professional audio, video, and photo documentation captured at every stop, it became a catalyst for the band's subsequent growth in Europe.
Tour July 2025

City of the Lost · 6 Cities

City of the Lost is an emerging indie band known for atmospheric soundscapes and emotive performances. In 2025, InfiniteLimits brought them to China for their first overseas tour, helping them build a dedicated following across 6 cities with full production and promotional support.

Guangzhou Shenzhen Hangzhou Shanghai Nanjing Beijing

Tour photos / 巡演照片

Highlights / 项目亮点

Despite having fewer than 500 followers on social media, strategic promotion and targeted marketing helped City of the Lost attract 150-250 audience members per show. The tour successfully established their presence in China and created a foundation for future international expansion.
Tour June 2026

UTBBYS · 6 Cities

UTBBYS is a post-rock band from Indonesia known for atmospheric soundscapes, hypnotic guitar layers, and emotionally charged instrumentals. In 2026, InfiniteLimits brought them to China for their debut overseas tour, delivering an immersive live experience across 6 cities.

Beijing Shanghai Guangzhou Shenzhen Hangzhou Chengdu

Tour photos / 巡演照片

Highlights / 项目亮点

UTBBYS's debut China tour introduced their cinematic post-rock sound to a new audience across 6 cities, with each show featuring carefully crafted visuals and lighting design that complemented their music. The band built a strong connection with Chinese listeners and established a foundation for future Asia tours.
