Tour August 2025

KWOON · 6 Cities

KWOON is a post-rock band from France known for intense live energy and genre-blending sound. In 2025, they released their new album Odyssey. InfiniteLimits organized and executed their album tour in China, across 6 cities with full technical production and merchandise support.

Beijing Shanghai Guangzhou Shenzhen Hangzhou Nanjing

Tour photos / 巡演照片

Highlights / 项目亮点

The tour featured professional sound and lighting engineers throughout, who worked with the band on effects before each show, and received widespread acclaim. With professional audio, video, and photo documentation captured at every stop, it became a catalyst for the band's subsequent growth in Europe.
