Russia / July 2024

Peremotka · China Tour

Peremotka is an independent electronic rock band from Russia, known for innovative stage performances and diverse musical styles. The tour expanded the band’s influence in China through local collaboration, special edition merchandise and immersive live production.

Beijing Shanghai Guangzhou Shenzhen Hangzhou Nanjing Wuhan Tianjin 8 Shows 500-800 / Show
Tour photos / 巡演照片
Highlights / 项目亮点
Targeted social media campaigns helped Peremotka grow from 17,000 to 50,000 NetEase Music followers after the tour. Laser lights and mushroom-shaped disco balls were integrated with the songs to create a distinctive live visual identity.

Sleep Dealer · China Tour

Sleep Dealer’s China tour covered major live music cities with consistent audience turnout and a dedicated merchandise line including colored vinyl, CD albums, cassette albums, eye masks and earplugs.

July 2025

City of the Lost · China Tour

This was City of the Lost’s first tour in China and their first large-scale overseas performance. Despite having fewer than 500 Instagram followers, focused promotion helped attract 150-250 audience members at each show.

Guangzhou Shenzhen Hangzhou Shanghai Nanjing Beijing 6 Shows 150-250 / Show

August 2025

KWOON · China Tour
