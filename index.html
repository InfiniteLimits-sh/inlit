About InfiniteLimits

Established in 2020, InfiniteLimits works across China’s live music ecosystem, supporting international artist tours, music-related merchandise partnerships and diverse live events in venues, festivals, arenas and art installations.

Since our inception, we have participated in over 500 live events, including 65 performances by international artists and nearly 20 exclusive artist showcases.
