About InfiniteLimits

Established in 2020, InfiniteLimits provides full-service China tour promoting and high-quality technical production for artists: from market analysis, venue booking, approval and documentation to live show execution and merchandise design.

We specialize in livehouse tours (4-10 stops per run), with proven technical experience across intimate venues, festivals, and arenas.
