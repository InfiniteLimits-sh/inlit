每一场演出的灯光与音效都至关重要

We treat the lighting and sound of every show with the utmost care.

Lighting Design

We provide experienced lighting engineers with the tour. From LiveHouse to festivals and concerts, we cover lighting design, programming, and on-site operation—all tailored to the band's musical style and creative needs, delivering complete stage production support for every show.

Sound Engineering

We provide seasoned sound engineers with the tour. They coordinate with venues in advance to confirm and secure all backline and technical requirements, and consult with the band before each show to lock in the desired tonal balance. This ensures not only a smooth soundcheck, but a consistent, well-crafted mix that translates on every stage.
